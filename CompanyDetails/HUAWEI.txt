Introduction to Huawei Technologies
Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd., founded in 1987 by Ren Zhengfei, is a multinational technology company headquartered in Shenzhen, Guangdong, China. From its humble beginnings as a reseller of private branch exchange (PBX) switches, Huawei has grown into one of the world’s largest providers of telecommunications equipment and information and communications technology (ICT) infrastructure.

Huawei operates in more than 170 countries and serves over three billion people globally. Despite facing geopolitical challenges and trade restrictions, Huawei has maintained its innovation-driven growth, becoming a leader in networking technologies, 5G infrastructure, consumer electronics, and cloud solutions.
